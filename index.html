How MISA Works

Private

No servers involved.

Your video moves directly between devices. Since nothing is ever stored on a server, your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

Speed

Direct device link.

Using a direct Wi-Fi or network link is much faster than traditional cloud uploads. We use every bit of your network's power.

No Quality Loss

Original file quality.

We don't compress your videos. You receive the exact same file that was sent, pixel for pixel.
