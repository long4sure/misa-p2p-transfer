How MISA Works

Private

No servers involved.

Your video moves directly between devices. Since nothing is ever stored on a server, your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

Speed

Direct device link.

Using a direct Wi-Fi or network link is much faster than traditional cloud uploads. We use every bit of your network's power.

No Quality Loss

Original file quality.

We don't compress your videos. You receive the exact same file that was sent, pixel for pixel.

Stay Active

Keep browser open.

MISA creates a live digital wire between devices. If you close the tab, lock your phone, or switch apps, the connection will drop.

Pro-Tips for Success

The DO's
  • Use high-speed Wi-Fi or local network.
  • Keep the tab active and screen awake.
  • Click 'Clear' after saving to free up RAM.
The DON'Ts
  • Don't send files larger than 2GB.
  • Don't refresh the page during transfer.
  • Don't switch to other apps on mobile.
