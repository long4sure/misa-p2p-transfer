# MISA (My Instant Sharing App) - Version Log

## [v2.0.0] - 2026-05-10
### "The Intelligence Update"
MISA has evolved from a basic P2P tool into a high-performance, assistant-guided gateway.

### 🚀 Performance & Reliability
- **Screen Wake Lock API**: Automatically prevents devices from sleeping during transfers.
- **256KB Reliable Chunking**: Optimized for multi-gigabyte video transfers.
- **Connection Lock**: 1-to-1 session security (prevents hijacking by other devices).
- **Asynchronous Optimization**: Refactored sender loop to prevent "double-indexing" bugs.

### 📱 Modern UX/UI
- **Enhanced MISA Assistant**: Advanced state-aware guidance and "Next Steps" logic.
- **Transfer History**: Privacy-first local log of your last 10 transfers.
- **Real-Time Metrics**: Live Speed (MB/s) and ETA (Seconds remaining) tracking.
- **QR Code Connectivity**: Generate and scan QR codes for instant device pairing.
- **Batch Actions**: Added "Save All" and "Clear All" for multi-file transfers.
- **Reverse-Chronological Ordering**: Most recently received videos now appear at the top.
- **Graceful Resets**: Friendly handshakes when restarting sessions.
- **Notification Badges**: Visual unread count for MISA messages.

### 💎 UX Enhancements
- **Next Steps Menu**: Post-transfer guidance (Start New / View History).
- **Notification Badges**: Visual unread count for MISA messages.
- **Friendly Error Handling**: Human-like responses for empty fields and typos.
- **Transfer History**: Local log of the last 10 activities (privacy-first storage).
